Introducing LockWidgets by ConorTheDev

Access your favourite widgets, directly on your lockscreen

The widgets feature on iOS is something we all take for granted, easy access to information from apps we all use day to day, on one swipe to the left on our home or lock screen.

What if you could see those widgets, in one simple glance without the need to swipe?

LockWidgets is a free and open source tweak that allows you to place any number of widgets from any application on your lock screen, meaning you no longer have to swipe across to see them bunched in with other widgets. When you select more than one widget, it displays in a Collection View which is easy to navigate and feels smooth, which integrates with the system.

LockWidgets is known to be very stable, providing you an awesome experience without major implications on your iDevice.

Important Notes:

  • LockWidgets is open source, if you wish to view the code you can check it out on GitHub

  • The icon and banner for LockWidgets was kindly designed by Ahmed Bousrih.

  • LockWidgets officially supports 13. iOS 12 is not supported in v2.0 at the moment.
