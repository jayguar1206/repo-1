ColorFlow 5 (iOS 13)

Supports iOS 13 - 13.5

For iOS 11 - 12.4 support see ColorFlow 4 (iOS 11 - 12)


Colorize your music! ColorFlow matches the now playing UI to your music's album artwork and adds in an iOS 10-style music player on the lock screen.

  • Colorize the lock screen's music player
    • Add in two new size modes: larger and full screen (iOS 10 style music player)
    • Add shuffle and repeat buttons for apps which support it (e.g. Music.app and Spotify)
  • Colorize the Music.app's now playing view and mini player view
  • Colorize Spotify's now playing view
    • Tested on Spotify 8.5.59

ColorFlow 5 supports all iOS 13 devices, including iPhones, iPads, and iPods.
Tweak Compatibility:

  • Artsy: Crashes have been reported with certain settings. Disable some Artsy features or uninstall it.
  • Notchless: Issues have been reported with the status bar coloring when ColorFlow's Resizing Mode is Full Screen, try changing it or uninstall Notchless
  • Slyph: Change ColorFlow's Resizing Mode to Default (no resizing) under the Lock Screen settings and respring

Configure ColorFlow 5 from Settings.
