dotto+
ConorTheDev & Mirac
Notifications, your style.

With dotto+, the big, bulky notification badges of stock iOS are replaced with minimalistic, unintrusive little red dots.

You can also customize the dots! Choose one of 11 hand picked colors or choose your own custom color with the color picker!

If you don't want to worry about colors enable "Adaptive Color" in dotto+'s settings. Our advanced algorithms will detect the color of your apps and adjust the color of the dots to make them match!

To keep up with progress on dotto+ and all of our other tweaks and themes, make sure to follow us on Twitter :)
