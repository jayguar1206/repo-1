Handcuff (iOS 13)
Tweaks

Catch who tries to unlock your device and steal your data!

Handcuff takes anonymous selfies when someone tries to unlock or power off your device. These selfies are sent to specified phone number and email address in form of SMS and Email.

Features:

  • Fully compatible with iOS 13
  • Save anonymous selfies to gallery when someone tries to unlock or power off your device
  • Send selfies to specified phone number as SMS
  • Send selfies to specified email address as Email
  • Set limit on passcode tries
  • Complete anonymous functionality
