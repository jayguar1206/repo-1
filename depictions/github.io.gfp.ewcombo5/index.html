EW.Combo.5
Lockscreen Widgets

EW.Combo.5 (for XenHTML)

Contains: 1 LS + 1 SB (iWidgets version also available)

*For iPhones only.

Requires XenInfo.

LS widget: Installed to the LockHTML directory. Apply with Xen HTML.

- Separated into 2 widgets: LS(bg) and LS(fore)

- Functions:

  • Tap the lower half of the image to toggle additional info view
  • Option added to always show the additional info view

- Setting up:

  • Apply LS(bg) as background widget
  • Apply LS(fore) as foreground widget
  • In "Notifications Behaviour" setting in XenHTML, switch on "Hide Foreground Widget for Notifications"
  • Set "Widget Opacity When Hidden" to 0 (the lowest)

- Widget settings/options:

  • Color & opacity of background
  • Color of texts
  • Use fingerprint image instead of "slide up" (additional fingerprint png also available in Stuff folder)
  • All images used in the screenshots are included in Stuff folder
  • How to use your own image? Place your image in /var/mobile/Library/LockHTML/EW.Combo.5_LS(fore)/Stuff, and rename it to Block1.png

HS widget: Installed to SBHTML/iWidgets directory. Apply with Xen HTML.

iWidgets could be used alongside FrontPage (e.g. mySB for icon placement). Widget.plist for all screen resolutions are provided:

  • Default sized for iPX/Xs/Xr (375 x 812)
  • XS Max (414 x 896)
  • iP8+/7+/6+ (414 x 736)
  • iP8/7/6 (375 x 667)
  • SE (320 x 568)

- Functions:

  • Tap the 1st Block to toggle alt info view
  • Tap the music icon in top right corner of 2nd Block to toggle music player
  • When in music player view, tap the small album arkword to launch the app playing music (e.g. apple music, spotify etc.)

- Widget settings/options:

  • Option to have more icon space
  • Color & opacity of background & icon holder
  • Color of texts
  • All images used in the screenshots are included in the Stuff folder
  • How to use your own image? Place your image in /var/mobile/Library/SBHTML or iWidgets/EW.Combo.5_SB or iW_new/Stuff, and rename it to Block1.png or Block2.png
