Sentinel
Tweak
Stop having to re-jailbreak your tethered jailbreak when your battery dies!

Sentinel is designed to help you avoid having to re-jailbreaking your device after running out of battery. When the battery charge reaches a set percentage it will initiate a fake shutdown.

TIP: If you want to escape the fake shutdown click the volume up button 3 times

How it works ?

When your device reaches a set percentage (3% by default) it will initiate a fake shutdown. This enables a factory tool called Hibernation also found in BatteryRamp. Along side that it also fully disables the springboard meaning notifications , alarms , calls , etc. Your phone will barely use any battery meaning you have more then enough time to reach a charger and you wont have to rejailbreak.
