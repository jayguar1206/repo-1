IntelligentPass 3

Everyone can relate to when you are home and Touch or Face ID refuses to work, or when you host a party and everyone asks for your passcode to queue their favorite songs. These situations can drive you crazy, or at least annoy you a bit.


IntelligentPass 3 allows you to disable your passcode on the lock screen when it is not necessary, but at the same time keep features like Apple Pay and iTunes purchases protected. It comes with a beautiful design and makes your device even more useful. IntelligentPass 3 is completely built with core functionality in iOS 13 in mind and supports both iPhones and iPads on iOS 13. The tweak can disable your passcode during events described down below.


Events
• Active Noise Control (ANC)
• IntelligentPass 3 can disable your passcode while your Airpods Pro are in the noise cancelling mode.


Airplane Mode
• IntelligentPass 3 can disable your passcode while in airplane mode.


Apple Watch
• IntelligentPass 3 can disable your passcode while connected to an Apple Watch.


Bluetooth Devices
• IntelligentPass 3 can disable your passcode when you are connected to a trusted bluetooth device.


CarPlay
• It can disable your passcode while you are connected to CarPlay.


Charging
• It can disable your passcode while charging your device.


Dark Mode
• It can disable your passcode while dark mode is enabled.


Do Not Disturb
• It can disable your passcode while your device has 'do not disturb' enabled.


Headphones
• IntelligentPass 3 can disable your passcode while connected to wired headphones.


Now Playing
• IntelligentPass 3 can disable your passcode while a certain app is playing music.


Time Interval
• IntelligentPass 3 can disable your passcode during certain time intervals, eg. from 6AM to 10AM.


WiFi Networks
• IntelligentPass 3 can disable your passcode when you are connected to your home network or other secure networks.


The best with IntelligentPass 3 is that it allows you to select which options above to enable. That way it makes your device even more unique!
