3DBadgeClear

3DBadgeClear is the Silzee Tweak of the Year Silver Award winner!

Thank you everyone!

Are you annoyed by those applications that keep showing badges on your home screen and cluttering it? Or do you just have way too many emails to deal with right now? Well, you don't have to worry about any of this anymore!

3DBadgeClear allows you to clear your badges from the Force Touch / 3D Touch menu! 3DBadgeClear also keeps the menu clean, as it will only appear on apps or folders that actually have a badge!

Compatible from iOS 10 to 13.3 and with support for both Light and Dark (system) mode! On older versions of iOS the tweak is compatible with Noctis and Eclipse. If you use any other tweak, please email me, I will add support for it!

Want to see this tweak in your language? Email me with the translation of "Clear Badge" in your language (make sure to not forget to tell me what's your language as well).

Follow me on Twitter for updates and more tweaks!

The icon has been kindly provided by @bossgfx_, give him a follow, he's an awesome designer! The screenshots are proudly using his beautiful theme, Viola!

Also thanks to @conorthedev for libconorthedev, this is what makes the tweak's Preferences so pretty!
