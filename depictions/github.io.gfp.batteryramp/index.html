  • Efficient Charging

    If you often forget to charge your phone before work or school and you need that extra juice quickly then BatteryRamp is the tweak for you!

    BatteryRamp is essentially an ultra power saving mode that disables elements of iOS temporarily to minimise power usage whilst charging.

    Usage: When you plug in your charger, a pop-up will appear asking if you would like to go into BatteryRamp mode and when you need to use your phone again all you need to do is simply press the volume up button 3 times or unplug your charger,

  • Fixed Issue where if you had a certain fast charger it would respring your device.
