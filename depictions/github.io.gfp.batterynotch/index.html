BatteryNotch
Tweaks

Compatible with iOS 11 - 13 on all notched devices.

Represent the battery precentage around the notch using a thin colored line that shrinks torwards the middle of the notch as the battery drains.

Colors can be applied from ColorFlow 5 while playing music!
Currently available options:

  • Choose a fixed color or have it automatically change from green to yellow to red (can be changed to any custom colors) as the battery drains.
  • Pulse the BatteryNotch bar when charging.
  • Use a thicker BatteryNotch bar.
  • Have a yellow color when in Low Power Mode.
  • Have a purple color when Do Not Disturb is active.
  • Make the BatteryNotch bar always full, great for users that wants to differentiate battery levels only using color or just showing off the notch with cool RGB colors.
  • Hide the stock battery indicator.
  • Added "Hide BatteryNotch" options for Lock screen, Home screen, Notification center and in applications.
