Info64 is an 80's terminal effect with detailed device informations in your lockscreen.

From today you can write your own lockscreen message with all the effects of the original tweak!!
This is a tribute for the "Intex Systems" in Alien Breed, © by Team 17.
Configure it on settings and double tap WITH 2 FINGERS in your lockscreen to show/hide.
