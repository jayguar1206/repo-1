ModernSettings
Tweaks

ModernSettings bring various modules to your settings app. Shuffle image cell, settingwidgets and dopesettings rolled in one. You can add modules, reorder them to your liking and scrolling various modules. You won't need to make a choice which widgets you want to use or the image cell, you can have it all with ModernSettings. Please ensure you check the compatible list before purchase this tweak.

Features

  • Photo canvas
  • WiFi and Cellular information (IP address, carrier name and MAC address)
  • Battery and storage
  • Connectivity and Power (Airplane mode, WiFi, Cellular, Bluetooth, Respring, Safemode, UICache and Reboot)
  • Device information contain various Information about your device, system uptime, country and currency
  • Customize Navigation Bar large title text
  • Customize individual cells titles text like dopesettings inside ModernSettings preference and you won't need to use Filza!
  • I'm planning to bring some modules from Hub to ModernSettings soon!

Compatible

  • iOS 13
  • iOS 12
  • iPhone SE (2nd generation), 6, 6 Plus, 6S, 6S Plus, 7, 7 Plus, 8, 8 Plus, X, XR, XS, XS Max, 11, 11 Pro and 11 Pro Max

Incompatible

  • iPhone SE (1st generation) and any devices below iPhone 6
  • iPad (will support soon)

  • Updated to latest build
