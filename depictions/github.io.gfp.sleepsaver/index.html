Add fully customisable screensavers to your iPhone, iPad or iPod!

Current features/options:
  • Full compatibility with iOS 11, 12 and 13.
  • Choose any video you want as your screensaver, straight from your camera roll.
  • Disable while Low Power Mode is enabled.
  • Disable while not charging.
  • Raise device to dismiss the screensaver.
  • Disable if the device's screen was woken by a notification.
  • Disable if music is being played.
  • Dismiss the screensaver when you "Raise To Wake".
  • Specify the duration of the fade-out animation.
  • Specify the amount of times you want the screensaver to be repeated. (There is also an infinite option).
  • Disables FaceID while the screensaver is showing. (Helps reduce battery drain).
