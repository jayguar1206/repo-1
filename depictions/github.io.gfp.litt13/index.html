Themes

When it comes to using an iOS device , I get frustrated as soon as I see my homescreen! It’s just too boring seeing those flat glyphs and ugly gradients. That’s the reason I make themes, and that’s my main reason to jailbreak my devices. With that in mind, I’m glad to release my 11th project since I started back in 2010, this is Litt13 in honor of iOS 13.

Note: I appreciate your support when you buy my themes, and I’m very great full to each and everyone of you who respect developers like me. I’m hopping I can raise enough to buy me an iPad Pro or at the very least an apple watch!
