ImLyingDownDamnit
A tweak to correct auto-rotate, so you can rotate your device freely, while lying down, relative to your face!

Feature-rich:

  • Auto-detect typical lying down behaviour to avoid prompting you
  • "Rollover" prompts, to correct the orientation if you change direction in bed!
  • Auto-disable during the afternoon (or any selected time)
  • Blacklist apps from showing popups
  • Disable rotate animations
  • Dark mode & haptics
  • And much, much, more...

Before use:

Ensure you have installed LibSparkAppList from this repo (Dynastic).
